Legs Workout Routine

Barbell Squats

Sets: 3

Reps: 6-8

Explanation: Barbell squats are a fundamental compound exercise that targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and lower back. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, hold the barbell across your upper back, and squat down by bending your knees and hips, then return to the standing position.

Romanian Deadlifts

Sets: 2

Reps: 8-10

Explanation: Romanian deadlifts primarily target the hamstrings and glutes. Stand with a barbell in front of you, grip it with an overhand grip, and, keeping your back straight, lower the barbell by pushing your hips back and bending slightly at the knees, then return to the starting position.

Leg Press

Sets: 2

Reps: 10-12

Explanation: The leg press targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. Sit on the leg press machine with your feet shoulder-width apart on the platform, push the platform away by extending your knees, then slowly return to the starting position.

Leg Curls

Sets: 2

Reps: 10-12

Explanation: Leg curls primarily target the hamstrings. Lie face down on the leg curl machine with your legs extended, curl your legs up by bending your knees, then lower them back to the starting position.

Calf Raises

Sets: 4

Reps: 8-10

Explanation: Calf raises target the calf muscles. Stand with the balls of your feet on the edge of a step or platform, raise your heels as high as possible by extending your ankles, then lower them back down.

Hanging Leg Raises

Sets: 2

Reps: 10-15

Explanation: Hanging leg raises target the lower abdominals. Hang from a bar with an overhand grip, raise your legs by flexing at the hips until they are parallel to the ground, then lower them back down.
